## 1. Team introduction
BPT Robotics is a young, three-member robotics team brought together by a shared
passion for technology, robotics, and innovation. We each contribute to our collaborative
work with distinct individual strengths.
Boldizsár is responsible for programming logic and artificial intelligence, building the
robot&#39;s &quot;brain.&quot; Peti is our expert in mechanical design and structural composition,
ensuring a stable and reliable chassis from initial CAD models all the way to the finished
3D-printed components. Tibi specializes in precise electronic connections, ensuring that
individual hardware components communicate flawlessly and receive the necessary
power supply.
Our robot performed successfully in the Hungarian qualifiers, which earned us a spot at
the 2025 WRO World Championship held in Slovenia.
Our objective is to present a technological solution demonstrating that significant results
can be achieved even with a small team and limited resources (both financial and
equipment-wise).
During the Slovenian competition in September, we encountered a couple of minor
issues, but we hope to have resolved them for this year&#39;s National Final. At that time, our
camera ordered during the summer had not yet arrived, but it reached us by December
and has since been integrated, enabling much faster and more accurate obstacle
detection.
Our motivation is to contribute to building a better future for our children and
grandchildren, and to participate in the most significant international robotics events at a
young age. It is no secret that all three of us wish to pursue careers in robotics and work
within this field in the future.
